Cookie Policy

Effective from October 2025

Transparency in how we collect, store, and use your browsing data.

1

Introduction

Welcome to Oryza Creative Agency ("Oryza," "we," "our," "us").

This Cookie Policy explains how and why cookies and similar technologies are used when you visit our website — www.oryza.design.

We believe in creative transparency and privacy — the same trust we expect from our clients.

2

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us improve performance, personalize your experience, and analyze website traffic.

There are two main types of cookies we use:

Session Cookies

Temporary files deleted after you close your browser.

Persistent Cookies

Stored for a set duration to remember preferences or login info.

3

How We Use Cookies

Oryza uses cookies to:

Enhance Performance

Enhance website performance and functionality.

Understand Visitors

Understand how visitors use our site for continuous improvement.

Personalize Content

Personalize content and service recommendations.

Enable Security

Enable secure client logins and session management.

Support Marketing

Support marketing and social media integrations.

Your data is never sold or misused — only used to enhance your creative experience.

4

Types of Cookies We Use

Type Purpose Example
Essential Cookies Enable core site features like navigation and login. Session tracking
Analytics Cookies Help us understand user interactions. Google Analytics
Functional Cookies Remember your preferences and settings. Language choice
Marketing Cookies Deliver relevant social media and advertising campaigns. Meta Pixel

6

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings:

Accept or Block

Accept or block cookies for specific websites.

Delete Cookies

Delete cookies anytime from your device.

Adjust Preferences

Adjust tracking preferences in your browser or via our cookie banner.

⚙️ Note: If you disable certain cookies, some features of the Oryza website may not function as intended.

7

Consent

By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

On your first visit, a cookie banner will appear allowing you to accept or customize your preferences.
